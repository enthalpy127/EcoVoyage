Time Plan for the trip:

Day 1

Swiss National Park

There's no way that this can be considered a successful trip without

paying a visit to the Swiss National Park in Zernez. That's why we

Ofenpass Area Nature Trail

Following on we have the Nature Trail between Zernaz
and the Munstertal Valley which provides intriguing

Day 2

Luke Lucerne Lake

Situated in central Switzerland is the fourth largest lake in
Switzerland, known for its unique shape, with countless
different bays and side arms.
Spend a whale of a time kayaking with your loved ones!

Mount Rigi

Mount Rigi is a popular tourist attraction for its surrounding
alps and lakes.
Also known as the "Queen of Mountains", Mount Rigi has
a highest height of a staggering 1798m.
