All you need is love and a passport

Givskud Zoo Zootopia

Enjoy a day in the animal kingdom – GIVSKUD ZOO is only a 25-minute drive from Billund. Take the family on safari in your own car or on the GIVSKUD ZOO Safari Bus. You drive across the African savannah, where zebras and giraffes have right of way. Continue into the Lions' Den and feel the thrill as the big cats lumber past your car. Throughout most of the season, the gorillas, lemurs and other species are fed daily. If you need to take a break, GIVSKUD ZOO has lots to offer the whole family – it's a great place to play, relax and have fun!

The Ancient Viking Stronghold of Aros

Take in the history and charming neighborhoods of Aarhus, the crown jewel of Jutland, on this affordable walking tour. Where you can learn about the ancient Viking settlement, once known as Aros, and discover how modern traffic signals demarcate where its boundaries once stood.

Tour with Odense River Cruise

Enjoy a beautiful trip in our tour boat on Odense Å. We sail from Odense City to the forest in Fruens Bøge. The whole trip is surrounded by the most beautiful beech trees and you do not find that you are in the center of Odense. You have the opportunity to disembark in the forest of Madame Bøge and go for a walk, enjoy a picnic basket or have lunch in one of the surrounding restaurants, and take a later departure back.

Street Art and Rooftops of Aarhus

Immerse yourself in the vibrant street art scene in Aaarhus on this walking tour. You'll pound the pavement with a guide to visit some of the best graffiti and street art, learning about the artists and the Danish art scene through guided commentary. Climb up to three local rooftops for panoramic views of the city and its landmarks with your small group of just 10 people or fewer.

SUP - Stand Up Paddle - Kitesurfing - Wingfoil & Kitefoil courses

Embrace the challenge of mastering your choice of water sport with a lesson in Varde, home to one of Denmark’s most scenic beaches. Choose to learn the techniques of either stand-up paddle boarding or kitesurfing, and receive personalized attention both in and out of the water in a small group limited to only 10 people

Explore the Instaworthy Spots of Aarhus with a Local

This experience is provided by an independent Local. If you want to spice up your Instagram feed and impress your friends with stunning shots from your travels, then you are at the right place! Let me show you the most beautiful and impressive spots in Aarhus and guide you with some useful tips to take good pictures from your phone or camera! Of course, while strolling through the city and taking photos of the most unique places, you will also get to know more about Aarhus and learn about its secrets from a local’s perspective. Join me on this adventure to give your insta feed an upgrade
